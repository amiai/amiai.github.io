HELLO

I'm a Melbourne-based designer with a focus on Product Design and UI/UX.

My background in Computer Science has provided me with knowledge and experience primarily in front-end development (React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS). Understanding how user experience and user interfaces can be translated into code is a skill that I believe is beneficial when collaborating cross-functionally, and can help provide a more improved end-result for users. I also enjoy and am well-grasped in working with teams that apply agile methodology and scrum concepts.

I'm passionate about creating inclusive, safe, and collaborative team environments. As a designer, I value empathetic approaches towards my users and my team.
