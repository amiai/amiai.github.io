I'm Amy and I'm currently studying Computer Science at RMIT University. I have
been mentored at SEEK and have gained industry knowledge surrounding UX
which has encouraged me to further my study. Through the study of my course,
I have also studied User-Centered Design which has deepened my knowledge
on UI/UX design as well as developing greater interest for it.

As a student surrounded by constantly new and emerging technology, I believe
that UI/UX design has become so much more noticeable and important than
ever before. And I believe that good UX can determine a user's opinion of the
brand and its message.
