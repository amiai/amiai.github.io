A little bit about me

Hello! I am a Melbourne-based designer with a main focus on UI/UX and product design. With a background in Computer Science, I have knowledge and experience primarily in front-end development (React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS). Understanding how user experience and the user interface can be translated into code is a skill that I believe would enable a smoother experience for the intended user. I also enjoy and am well-grasped in working with teams working in agile environments. My previous team experience involving the application of agile concepts through scrum processes has enabled me the ability to better understand and utilise my knowledge throughout various stages of the development process.
